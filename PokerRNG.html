Poker Strategy Randomizer

Looking to randomize your online poker decisions? Use this tool to assist in implementing a mixed strategy. Here's how:
  1. Choose Your Strategy: Suppose you decide to employ a mixed strategy, like betting 70% of the time and checking 30%.
  2. Select the Appropriate Button: Click on the button that corresponds to your primary action's probability (in this case, 70% for betting).
  3. View the Decision: The display will indicate "YES" (meaning take the primary action, e.g., bet) or "NO" (opt for the alternative, e.g., check).
Using this randomizer, you can ensure you're sticking to your strategy percentages and keeping your gameplay unpredictable!
